# ADR-0001: Use ESP-IDF, Not Arduino

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Use ESP-IDF as the official firmware framework.

## Reason

ESP-IDF provides better control over FreeRTOS, OTA, partitioning, networking, watchdogs, and production-grade build flow.

## Consequences

- Higher learning curve
- Better long-term maintainability
- Better commercial readiness
